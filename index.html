The Doctrinal Foundation

Al-Mu'dhiyat & Fawasiq

The 5 creatures named in Sahih al-Bukhari and Sahih Muslim that may be killed even in the sacred precincts of the Haram.

خَمْسٌ مِنَ الدَّوَابِّ كُلُّهُنَّ فَاسِقٌ يُقْتَلْنَ فِي الْحَرَمِ: الْغُرَابُ وَالْحِدَأَةُ وَالْعَقْرَبُ وَالْفَأْرَةُ وَالْكَلْبُ الْعَقُورُ

"Five are the vicious and harmful creatures which may be killed even in the state of Ihram: the crow, the kite, the scorpion, the mouse, and the rabid dog."

Sahih Muslim 1198 • Sahih al-Bukhari 1828

The Scientific Integration

Integrated Pest Management

Modern epidemiology and entomology validate these rulings. The creatures identified in scripture are primary vectors of lethal zoonotic pathogens (Rabies, Leptospirosis, Plague) or possess dangerous venom.

Our platform merges these traditions, advocating for structural exclusion, sanitation, and humane targeted elimination instead of indiscriminate mass extermination.
