Ethical Pest Control & Animal Rights

Islam forbids cruelty to animals. Even when a creature is permitted to be killed due to its harm, the Prophet ﷺ mandated *Ihsan* (excellence and compassion) in the act.

❌ Prohibited Killing Methods:

  • • No Burning: Fire is strictly prohibited. You must never burn ant nests or insect colonies.
  • • No Boiling: Pouring boiling water into walls or holes is forbidden.
  • • No Starvation/Glue Boards: Glue boards trap rodents and bugs alive to starve to death slowly. Use quick snap traps instead.
  • • No Unnecessary Killing: If a creature can be relocated without harm or risk, killing it is disliked.

✓ Approved Humane Methods (Ihsan):

Select methods that guarantee swift, painless death or secure exclusion:

  • • Instant crushing / mechanical strike (e.g. fly swatters, heavy boots, snap traps)
  • • Electronic traps (high voltage instant culling)
  • • Non-burning pesticides (target sprays)
  • • Secure physical barriers to block entries
