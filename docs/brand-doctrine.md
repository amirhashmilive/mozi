# MOZI — Brand Doctrine & Identity System

MOZI operates at the intersection of classical Islamic jurisprudence and modern biosecurity. It is a premium, scholarly, and scientific platform. It must never look or feel like a blog, a cheap pest-control sales page, or a religious polemic site.

---

## 1. Brand Tone & Voice

*   **Scholarly:** Treat texts with classical academic rigor. Use precise vocabulary.
*   **Clinical & Scientific:** Present biological facts, entomology, and medical details with objectivity.
*   **Cinematic & Premium:** The visual style is premium dark-mode, leveraging glassmorphic cards, emerald-green alerts, and clean typography.
*   **Doctrinally Anchored:** Every statement concerning Islamic law must trace back to primary sources or consensus.

---

## 2. Core Terminology Guide

*   **Al-Mu'dhiyāt (Harmful Creatures):** The overarching category of animals, insects, or pests that cause harm (*dharar*) to human life, health, property, or food supplies.
*   **Fawāsiq (Transgressors):** The specific group of 5 (or 6) creatures explicitly named by the Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) as permissible to kill even within the sacred precincts of Makkah (*Haram*) due to their inherent harm.
*   **Ihsān (Excellence/Humaneness):** The theological requirement to act with kindness and efficiency. In the context of pest control, this means selecting the quickest, least painful method of elimination.
*   **Khalīfah (Stewardship):** The concept of human stewardship over the Earth. This mandates preserving ecological balances and not exterminating species indiscriminately.

---

## 3. Visual & Creative Direction

*   **Image Direction:** Visual assets should resemble dark biological illustrations, national-history archive plates, or clinical diagrams. Avoid cartoons, horror-movie mockups, or low-quality stock photography.
*   **Ambient Glows:** Use HSL variables to cast soft emerald-green shadows for permissible/scientific components, and deep blue-indigo shadows for protected species.
*   **Typography:** The font is strictly `Inter` (sans-serif) for both headings and body copy, giving a modern, premium feel.

---

## 4. Prohibited Language & Content

*   *Do not use:* "Pest infestation nightmare", "kill them all", "disgusting bugs", "demonic creatures", "pest apocalypse".
*   *Do use:* "Vector population containment", "mitigation protocol", "doctrinal status: permissible", "ecological role".
*   *No torture:* Never describe or show methods of burning, boiling, or gluing creatures to starve.
*   *Disclaimers:* Every page describing venomous or disease-carrying creatures must prominently display the standardized medical disclaimer.
