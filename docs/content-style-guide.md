# MOZI — Content & Style Guide

This style guide ensures typographic consistency and structural uniformity across all content modules on the MOZI platform.

---

## 1. Transliteration Standard

Since the platform is strictly Roman English end-to-end, all Arabic and Sanskrit/Urdu terminology must use a consistent transliteration system:

*   **Prophetic Honorific:** Use `(peace be upon him)` or the transliterated abbreviation `(sallallahu 'alayhi wa sallam)` when appropriate.
*   **The Arabic definite article:** Write as `al-` (lowercase) unless it begins a sentence, and use sun-letter contraction rules (e.g., *al-Mu'dhiyat*, *Sahih al-Bukhari*, *mina'd-dawabbi*).
*   **Vowels & Diacritics:** Long vowels should be marked with macron accents if possible (ā, ī, ū) or standard Roman letters (a, i, u) for compatibility. High-frequency keywords must be italicized:
    *   *Al-Mu'dhiyāt*
    *   *Fawāsiq*
    *   *Ihsān*
    *   *Mīzān*

---

## 2. Creature Naming Convention (Hinglish)

To connect with South Asian audiences, every creature record must feature its Hinglish/vernacular name in Roman script alongside the English and scientific names:

| English Name | Hinglish (Romanized) | Latin / Scientific |
|---|---|---|
| Scorpion | Bichhu | *Scorpiones* |
| Snake | Saanp | *Serpentes* |
| Mosquito | Machchar | *Culicidae* |
| Cockroach | Tilchatta | *Blattodea* |
| Rat | Chuha | *Rattus* |
| Termite | Deemak | *Isoptera* |

---

## 3. Hadith Citation Format

All Hadiths must be cited systematically:
`[Collection Name] [Book Name/Chapter Title], Hadith [Number]`

*Example:*
> "Five are the transgressive creatures that may be killed in the sacred precinct..." — *Sahih al-Bukhari, Kitab al-Hajj, Hadith 1829*

All Hadith panels must render the Arabic source text as an italicized Roman transliteration followed by the English translation.

---

## 4. Medical & Legal Disclaimer Placement

A medical warning card must be rendered in a prominent crimson border on the third slide ("Health Risks & Emergency") of every creature's detail guide and at the footer of the emergency response panels.

*Disclaimer Template:*
> **Medical Disclaimer:** The information provided here is for educational and public awareness purposes only. In the event of a bite, sting, or severe exposure, immediately contact local emergency services (108) or go to the nearest hospital. Do not rely on home remedies.
