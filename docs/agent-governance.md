# MOZI — Agent Governance & Safety Standards

This document establishes the mandatory safety, doctrinal, and scientific boundaries that any AI agent or content creator must adhere to when modifying or extending the MOZI platform.

---

## 1. Core Mandates (Non-Negotiable)

*   **No Hadith Fabrication:** Never invent, modify, or paraphrase a Hadith to suit a ruling. Every Hadith must be quoted accurately with its primary source (e.g., Sahih al-Bukhari, Sahih Muslim, Sunan Abi Dawud) and citation number.
*   **No Fiqh Fabrication:** Never invent or extrapolate a fiqh (jurisprudential) ruling. All rulings must be explicitly attributed to the established schools of Islamic jurisprudence (Hanafi, Maliki, Shafi'i, Hanbali) or documented scholarly consensus.
*   **No Medical/Scientific Fabrication:** Never fabricate clinical advice, first aid steps, or epidemiology statistics. All medical recommendations must align with verified public health bodies (WHO, ICMR, CDC).
*   **No Sensationalism or Terror Aesthetics:** Do not use horror imagery, graphic descriptions, or alarmist text. The platform is a clinical, scholarly, and biosecurity archive.
*   **No Animal Cruelty:** Do not advocate for methods that cause prolonged suffering, torture, or burning of creatures. The Prophetic command of *Ihsan* (excellence/humaneness) in all actions must be upheld.
*   **Roman English Script Only:** All content, titles, headings, and data fields must be rendered in Roman English or Romanized transliteration. Do not output Arabic script, Devanagari script, or RTL formatting on the client-facing website to maintain cross-platform typographic consistency.

---

## 2. Hadith Authenticity Tiers

All Hadith citations used on the platform must be categorized under one of the following tiers:

1.  **Tier 1: Mutawatir / Sahihayn (Highest)**
    *   Found in *Sahih al-Bukhari* or *Sahih Muslim*.
    *   These form the basis of explicit prohibitions or permissions.
2.  **Tier 2: Sunan (Authenticated)**
    *   Found in *Sunan Abi Dawud*, *Sunan al-Tirmidhi*, *Sunan al-Nasa'i*, or *Sunan Ibn Majah* and graded as *Sahih* (authentic) or *Hasan* (good) by classical and contemporary scholars.
3.  **Tier 3: Weak (Da'if) — Prohibited for Rulings**
    *   Weak Hadiths must not be used to justify legal permissions to kill or protect any creature.

---

## 3. Content Verification Checklist

Before deploying any new creature page or data file, verify:

- [ ] Is the creature's scientific classification (family, genus, species) accurate?
- [ ] Are the vector transmission routes for diseases medically validated?
- [ ] Is the primary Hadith cited for this creature authentic?
- [ ] Are the positions of all four Sunni Madhabs represented accurately based on classical reference texts (e.g., *Al-Mughni*, *Al-Majmu'*, *Fath al-Bari*)?
- [ ] Does the prevention section rely on Integrated Pest Management (IPM) rather than solely chemical or destructive methods?
- [ ] Does the copy render completely in Roman script (no raw Arabic or Hindi characters)?
