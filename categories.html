Classification System

Creature Categories

MOZI classifies creatures into 5 categories based on their primary mode of harm and Islamic jurisprudential status.

⚠ Venomous

Creatures that inject venom through stings or bites, causing pain, tissue damage, or death.

Scorpion Snake Wasp Centipede Venomous Spider

🦠 Disease Vectors

Creatures that transmit bacterial, viral, or parasitic diseases to humans.

Mosquito Cockroach Housefly Flea Tick Bed Bug

🚧 Property Destroyers

Creatures that damage buildings, food stores, books, electrical wiring, or other property.

Rat Termite Carpenter Ant Pigeon

🏠 Household Intruders

Creatures that enter homes and pose health or safety risks through their presence.

Stray Dog Bat Gecko

💚 Protected — Do Not Kill

Creatures explicitly protected by the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ or given special status in the Qur'an. Killing these is prohibited (haram) or strongly discouraged.

Frog Hoopoe Bee Ant Harmless Spider
