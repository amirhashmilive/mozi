        Ecological Perspective

        Qiyamah & Ecology

        وَمَا مِن دَابَّةٍ فِي الْأَرْضِ وَلَا طَائِرٍ يَطِيرُ بِجَنَاحَيْهِ إِلَّا أُمَمٌ أَمْثَالُكُم

        "There is no creature on earth, nor a bird that flies with its wings, but they are communities like you."

        Surah Al-An'am 6:38

        Every creature exists within a divinely ordained ecological balance. The Qur'an emphasizes that all living beings form communities (umam) with purpose and place in creation.

        The story of Prophet Sulaiman (عليه السلام) and the ant colony teaches us that even the smallest creatures have awareness, communication, and rights.

        Islam mandates a balance: eliminate genuine threats to human safety, but never engage in indiscriminate killing, torture, or ecological destruction.
